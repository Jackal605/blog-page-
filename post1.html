Learning Japanese writing is a fascinating journey that involves mastering three different scripts: Hiragana, Katakana, and Kanji. Each script has its own unique characteristics and uses. Hiragana is used for native Japanese words, Katakana is primarily used for foreign words, and Kanji consists of characters borrowed from Chinese. Understanding these scripts is essential for reading and writing in Japanese.
